Beulah Beach Whiteout

February 18-20

 

Youth groups from across our diocese will be going to Beulah Beach's Whiteout retreat in February. It's a chance to get away, worship, learn about God and faith, and have a ton of fun. We'll have the chance to meet a bunch of other students from all over the Midwest, and also to build up connections within our group. You can find out more about the retreat at their website here. (Also - there is a waterpark!)

To sign up, let Andrew know by December 13th so he can add your name to the group registration. The necessary forms will be sent out after that.
